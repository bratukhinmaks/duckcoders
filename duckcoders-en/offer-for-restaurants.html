What do we offer to restaurants?

Regarding the operation of the restaurant during the quarantine, we would like to offer you a solution. There are Pyszne, Uber, Wolt, Glovo services, but why do you have to pay them a commission? We can offer solution: website + shop (with online payment option). This means that you will pay once and you will not have to pay a percentage of the orders each time. And your customers will be able to order even during quarantine.
